Timeline.

I am, what I am.

Alyssa is an artist that was born in Riverside, California. She moved with her family at 13 to Colorado, where she finished her dual Bachelor's degree while bartending full-time. Bartending became more of a focus after college, but her passion for creativity continued. She later found an interest in web design and web development, so she began her journey of self-learning. Alyssa is now a full-time Bartender at Mariott International, with the hopes of becoming a full-time freelancer so she can continue to explore her creativity!
